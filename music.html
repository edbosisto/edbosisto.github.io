Music

A passion for playing and recording music

Background

I'm a self taught guitarist and vocalist and have spent my life playing musical instruments of all shapes and sizes, from violin to piano and even the bagpipes.
Acoustic rock music is my preferred genre, but I'll listen to anything. I love covering my favourite songs and have also written, recorded and released songs of my own.
